Custom Designs

Have a specific circuit in mind? Want to translate a classroom schematic into a hands-on lab experience? We offer 1:1 collaboration with professors and instructors to create custom miniPCB™ designs tailored to your course content.

Whether you're teaching filters, analog-to-digital conversion, switching regulators, or embedded systems, we can help turn your educational objectives into compact, clearly labeled, testable PCBs your students can build, measure, and explore.

We welcome early-stage ideas and sketched concepts. You’ll receive design previews and can give input throughout the process.

If you're interested in working together, please get in touch with a brief description of your course and what kind of board you’d like to create. We’ll take it from there.

Let’s design something that helps your students learn better—together.
