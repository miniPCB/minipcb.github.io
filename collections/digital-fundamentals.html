Digital Fundamentals Collection

The Digital Fundamentals Collection is a set of educational circuit boards designed to support first- and second-semester electronics courses focused on digital circuit design and operation.

This collection includes eight foundational digital circuit configurations:

  • Non-Inverting Schmitt Trigger
  • Inverting Schmitt Trigger
  • Pulse Generator
  • D Flip-Flop
  • T Flip-Flop
  • NAND Gate
  • NOR Gate

Each circuit is implemented as a standalone miniPCB™ with standardized layout, clear labeling, and accessible test points, making them easy to incorporate into digital lab environments. These boards are ideal for reinforcing key learning outcomes such as logic-level thresholds, signal integrity, propagation delay, and the fundamentals of digital interfacing.

Instructors can use the full set to guide students through a structured progression—from basic inverters and buffers to Schmitt triggers, gated circuits, and multistage logic functions—while encouraging hands-on exploration of digital behavior, timing characteristics, and circuit response under various loading conditions.
