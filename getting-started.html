Recommended Component Kits

To assemble and test miniPCB™ boards, you'll need basic through-hole components. The kits below contain resistors, capacitors, transistors, and ICs that are commonly used in our designs.

  • ELEGOO Resistor Kit (0Ω–1MΩ, 1/4W, ±1%) – Amazon
  • Electrolytic Capacitor Assortment (0.1µF–1000µF) – Amazon
  • NPN/PNP Transistor Kit – Amazon
  • Assorted Opamps (DIP Package) – eBay
  • Trimmer Potentiometers – Amazon

Also recommended: soldering iron, wire cutters, multimeter, and jumper wire.
