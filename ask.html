Asking for Help

Are you an instructor, professor, or educator? I'd greatly appreciate your help evaluating these miniPCB™ boards in a real classroom or lab environment. I'm currently offering free miniPCB™ samples for academic use—completely free of charge, including shipping.

If you're willing, please solder up a board or two, power them up, and put them through their paces. I’d love to hear how they perform in your hands. Even better—send back a few photos of the boards in action, whether on the bench or in the classroom.

I feature educator feedback directly in each board’s datasheet—for example, see the contribution from Professor Goncalo at the University of Denver in the Common Emitter Amplifier datasheet . If you post about the boards on your department or personal website, I’d be glad to link to it from the corresponding miniPCB™ product page.

To request your free samples, just send me an email with your name, university, mailing address, and a brief note about which boards you'd like to try.
