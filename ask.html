Asking for Help

If you're an instructor, professor, or educator, I'd love your help testing these boards in a real lab setting. I'm currently offering free miniPCB™ samples for classroom or individual evaluation—no cost, including shipping.

If you’re willing, please take a moment to solder up a board or two, power them up, and see how they perform. Then share your feedback—and if possible, send back a few photos of the board in action or in the lab.

I feature industry feedback directly in each board’s datasheet—like the feedback from Professor Goncalo at University of Denver in the Common Emitter Amplifier's datasheet. If you publish content related to the board on your personal or department website, I’ll gladly link to it from the respective miniPCB™ product page.

To request samples, just email me with your name, University, mailing address, and a note about which boards interest you.
