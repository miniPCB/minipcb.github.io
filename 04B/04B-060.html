04B-060 – Common Base Amplifier

A foundational BJT amplifier for teaching gain, biasing, and small-signal response.

PCB Details Part No: 04B-060
Title: Common Base Amplifier
Pieces per Panel: 4
Board Size: Approx. 50 x 50 mm
Panel Size: 100 x 100 mm (V-scored)
Key Learning Objectives
  • Understand the operation and biasing of a common base BJT configuration
  • Explore the low input impedance and high voltage gain characteristics
  • Analyze the amplifier’s current gain and voltage transfer behavior
  • Compare performance with common emitter and other amplifier types
  • Perform small-signal measurements to validate theoretical predictions
