09H-13 – Buck (Step-Down) Converter (MC34063)

A compact boost converter design for efficient voltage step-up applications.

Circuit Description

This miniPCB implements a buck (step-down) converter built around the MC34063 controller (U1). The board accepts a DC input at P1-1 (V+) and provides a lower regulated output at P1-5.

Power Input & Filtering

  • C1/C2/C3 provide bulk and high-frequency bypassing for the input rail.
  • R1 (sense) feeds the MC34063’s current-limit input to protect the switch.

Switching Stage

  • The internal switch of U1 drives the inductor L1 (pads for an optional second footprint L2).
  • D1 is the catch diode that conducts during switch off-time.
  • C5/C6 smooth the output ripple; C4 sets the MC34063 oscillator frequency.

Regulation & Feedback

  • R2/R3 form the feedback divider that sets the output voltage (adjust by ratio).
  • LED1 (with series resistor) provides a visual output-present indicator.

Connections

  • P1-1: Ground (GND) reference for input and output.
  • P1-2: Positive DC input (V+).
  • P1-3: No connection (NC).
  • P1-4: Positive DC input (V+), internally linked to P1-2.
  • P1-5: Regulated DC output (Vout).

Typical use: step a higher DC supply down to a lower, regulated rail for lab experiments. Choose inductor value, diode type (Schottky recommended), and output capacitors to meet your load current and ripple targets.
