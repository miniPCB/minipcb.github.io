About miniPCB™

miniPCB™ is a growing collection of small, purpose-built circuit boards created to support hands-on learning in electronics education. Each board is designed to make it easier for students to explore real-world circuits, test ideas, and develop confidence in their skills.

How It Started

My journey began in a university lab, where I saw firsthand how students struggled to connect theory with real circuits. Schematic diagrams on a projector couldn’t compete with the insight gained from probing a real, working board.

I began designing small PCBs for common circuit types—amplifiers, filters, oscillators—hoping to bridge the gap between lecture and lab. The feedback from instructors was encouraging, and the boards quickly evolved into a full-fledged educational toolset.

What Makes miniPCBs Unique

  • Standardized layout and labeling across all designs
  • Built-in test points to encourage measurement and exploration
  • Compact 100 × 100 mm v-scored panels for easy distribution
  • Support for both analog and digital curriculum topics

Who It's For

miniPCBs are built with educators in mind. Whether you're teaching introductory electronics or advanced analog design, these boards are meant to complement your curriculum, reinforce key concepts, and spark hands-on curiosity in the classroom.

Looking Ahead

I’m continuously adding new designs based on instructor feedback and evolving classroom needs. I also welcome collaborations for custom boards built specifically for your courses.
