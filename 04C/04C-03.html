The 04C-03 is a compact 4‑channel low‑side driver board for switching DC loads from microcontrollers or PLC logic. Each channel uses a logic‑level MOSFET with input buffering, supporting 3.3 V and 5 V control. Integrated clamp diodes and RC damping protect against inductive kickback and help reduce EMI.

Operation: connect IN1–IN4 to digital outputs, share ground with the controller, and feed the load supply (typically 5–24 V) to the V+ terminal. The board sinks current on OUT1–OUT4 to switch loads; status LEDs mirror channel state. Per‑channel current capacity suits small relays, solenoids, valves, and LED strings; observe thermal limits and derate for continuous duty.

Standard 2.54 mm header and 3.5 mm screw terminals simplify wiring. Solder jumpers allow selection of input pull‑up/down biasing and LED enable. Use 04C-03 as a clean interface between low‑voltage control logic and field devices in test fixtures, panels, and compact automation.

  • 4 independent low‑side MOSFET switches, logic‑level driven
  • 3.3/5 V compatible inputs with buffering and basic ESD protection
  • Typical load supply 5–24 V; shared ground required
  • Integrated flyback diodes; status LEDs per channel
  • 0.1 in (2.54 mm) header plus 3.5 mm screw terminals
